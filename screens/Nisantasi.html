About My Academic Journey at Nişantaşı University

Throughout my academic journey at Nişantaşı University, I have strived to excel in my studies and gain valuable experiences that have shaped my educational path. My time at the university can be divided into several key phases, each contributing to my growth and development as a student.

Beginning of My Academic Journey

My academic journey commenced in the 2020-2021 academic year, where I began my studies with the Hazırlık-I (Y) program. During this preparatory year, I focused on building a strong foundation for my future studies. Although my grades during this period were not reflected in GPA points, this phase provided me with essential skills and knowledge to succeed in my subsequent years.

First Official Academic Year

Moving on to my first official academic year, which was the 2021-2022 academic year, I embarked on my undergraduate journey with great enthusiasm. During the fall semester, I took a variety of courses, including "Communication Skills and Academic Reporting I," "Physics I," "Mathematics I," "Ataturk's Principles and History of Revolution I," and "Introduction to Software Engineering." This semester laid the groundwork for my academic endeavors and introduced me to fundamental concepts in my field of study.

Continuing My Academic Progress

In the following spring semester of 2021-2022, I continued to expand my knowledge with courses like "Communication Skills and Academic Reporting II," "Physics II," "Chemistry," "Mathematics II," "Ataturk's Principles and History of Revolution II," and "Introduction to Computer Programming."

Entering the Third Phase

During the 2022-2023 academic year, I entered the third phase of my academic journey. This year, I delved deeper into my chosen field with courses like "Differential Equations," "Linear Algebra," "Software Requirements and Analysis," "Object-Oriented Programming," "Probability and Statistics," and "Turkish Language I."

Looking Forward to the Future

In the fourth phase of my academic journey, which took place during the spring semester of 2022-2023, I continued to make progress. I undertook courses such as "Communication Skills and Academic Reporting II," "Physics II," "Mathematics II," "Ataturk's Principles and History of Revolution II," "Formal Language and Automata Theory," and "Turkish Language II."

In Summary

In summary, my journey at Nişantaşı University has been a transformative experience. I have not only gained a strong academic foundation but also developed important life skills such as time management, critical thinking, and perseverance. I look forward to completing my education at Nişantaşı University and using the knowledge and skills I have acquired to make a positive impact in my chosen field.
